## Tranportation  
  <a href="https://www.michiganflyer.com/" target="_blank">**Michigan Flyer Airport Motorcoach**</a>  
    Plan your arrival and departure time accordingly as hours of operation are limited.   

  <a href="https://www.theride.org/" target="_blank">**Ann Arbor Bus System**</a>  
    Single fare can be bought ahead of time online or by paying cash or token when boarding the bus.  
    <a href="https://www.theride.org/fares-passes/buy-online" target="_blank">Buy a 1-Day Pass</a> for unlimited rides. Check the website for route details.   

  <a href="https://mbus.ltp.umich.edu/map" target="_blank">**University of Michigan Magic Bus**</a>  
    Blue Buses are free to all students and guests on campus. Check the website for route details. 


  **Rideshare: <a href="https://www.lyft.com/" target="_blank">Lyft</a> | <a href="https://www.uber.com/" target="_blank">Uber</a>**  
    Avoid rideshare to and from the airport as it can be expensive.

## Local Culture Highlights  
  Check out Kerrytown, search for fairy doors around Downtown, or take a casual stroll around the many Ann Arbor parks.  
  Wander the University of Michigan campus, stop by at the free University of Michigan museums and try the free University of Michigan Blue Buses.   

## Weather  
  Michigan autumns mark cool temperatures and occasional rainfall.  
  Variable temperatures thoughout the day, so layers are key!    
  Monitor the weather forecast closer to the wedding date for accurate clothing preparation. 

## Parking  
  Ample parking will be available at event locations.
